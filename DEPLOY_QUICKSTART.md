## Manual Deployment

1. **Fork this repository** on GitHub
2. **Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com)** and sign in
3. **Click "New Project"**
4. **Import your forked repository**
5. **Click "Deploy"** (Vercel auto-detects Vite)
